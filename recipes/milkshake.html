Chocolate Mint Milkshake

Description

This cool, refreshing chocolate mint milkshake recipe will have you craving for more. All you need is milk, vanilla ice cream, chocolate syrup, and a few drops of peppermint extract.

Ingredients

  • 4 scoops vanilla ice cream
  • ¼ cup milk
  • ¼ cup chocolate syrup
  • 1 drop peppermint extract
